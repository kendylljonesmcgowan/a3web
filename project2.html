LiDAR and Bathymetry Services

  • Topographic LiDAR

    Topographic LiDAR allows the direct measurement of the ground surface and objects above the ground, providing accurate elevation models of all ground types, no matter how dense the vegetation is in a given area. The 3D model created by topographic LiDAR provides information on the ground elevation as well as objects on the ground. Allowing topography, built structures, and vegetation all to be mapped precisely in three dimensions.
